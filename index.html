ML4chemLA

This project aims to train students and researchers from developing countries to use open data science. We organize workshops for individuals without programming knowledge. We focus on increasing the availability of resources for Spanish and Portuguese speakers, to broaden access to open data science in Latin America. You can check our previous workshop here.
